Cranberry Orange Glazed Ham

2 hrs • Easy • 4.8★

Ingredients

  • 1 fully cooked bone-in ham (about 8–10 lbs)
  • 1 cup cranberry sauce (whole berry)
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Fresh rosemary or orange slices for garnish (optional)
0:00 0:00

Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C).

Place the ham in a roasting pan, flat side down.

In a small saucepan, combine cranberry sauce, orange juice, brown sugar, mustard, cloves, and cinnamon.

Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring frequently, until well combined.

Score the ham in a crisscross pattern.

Brush generously with half of the glaze.

Cover with foil and bake for 1.5 hours.

Remove foil, brush on remaining glaze, and bake uncovered for 30 minutes.

Let rest before slicing.

Garnish with fresh rosemary and orange slices for a festive touch.

Happy Holidays!
