Gingerbread Cookies

2 hrs • Beginner • 4.8★

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup molasses
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
0:00 0:00

In a large bowl, beat together butter and brown sugar until creamy.

Add molasses and egg, and mix well.

In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, salt, and spices.

Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture.

Form dough into a disk, wrap in plastic, and chill for 1 hour.

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

Roll out dough on a floured surface to 1/4-inch thick.

Cut into shapes using cookie cutters.

Place on baking sheet and bake for 8–10 minutes.

Cool completely before decorating.

Enjoy your festive gingerbread cookies!
