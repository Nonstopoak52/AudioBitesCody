Rainbow Vegan Bowl

25 min • Vegan • 4.8★

Ingredients

  • 1 block firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber slices
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup lettuce, chopped
  • ½ avocado, sliced
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (optional)
  • Dressing of your choice (tahini or peanut recommended)
0:00 3:00

Start by tossing cubed tofu with soy sauce and cornstarch.

Heat sesame oil in a pan and fry the tofu until golden brown on all sides.

Set aside to cool.

In a large bowl, arrange lettuce, cabbage, carrots, cucumbers, tomatoes, and avocado in sections around the bowl.

Place the tofu right in the center.

Drizzle with your favorite dressing and sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired.
