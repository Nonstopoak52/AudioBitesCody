Strawberry Cheesecake

1.5 hrs • Intermediate • 4.9★

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 cups cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 1/4 cup strawberry glaze or jam
0:00 0:00

Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).

Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter.

Press into a springform pan to form the crust.

In a bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.

Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each.

Mix in vanilla and sour cream.

Pour filling over the crust and smooth the top.

Bake for 1 hour, then turn off oven and leave inside for 30 minutes.

Cool completely and chill for 4 hours.

Top with strawberry glaze and fresh strawberries.

Slice and enjoy!
