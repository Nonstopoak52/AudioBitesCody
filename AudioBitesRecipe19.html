Macaroons

45 min • Intermediate • 4.9★

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 3/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 large egg whites (room temp)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Buttercream or ganache filling of choice
0:00 0:00

Sift together almond flour and powdered sugar.

In a separate bowl, whip egg whites until foamy.

Gradually add granulated sugar and beat to stiff peaks.

Fold dry ingredients into egg whites gently.

Add food coloring if desired.

Pipe small rounds onto a baking sheet lined with parchment.

Let sit for 30 minutes to form a skin.

Bake at 300°F for 15-17 minutes.

Cool, fill, and sandwich. Enjoy!
