Emeril Lagasse's Shrimp Scampi

30 minutes • Seafood • 5★

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Sauté garlic in butter and oil.

Add shrimp and wine.

Stir in lemon juice and red pepper.

Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
