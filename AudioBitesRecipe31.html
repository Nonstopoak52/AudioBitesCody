Spicy Mango Salsa

15 mins • Easy • 5★

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangos (diced)
  • 1/4 cup red onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small red chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup cilantro (chopped)
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
0:00 0:00

Combine mango, red onion, chili, and cilantro in a bowl.

Squeeze lime juice over the mixture, then sprinkle with cumin and salt.

Mix gently until combined.

Serve with tortilla chips or grilled meats.
