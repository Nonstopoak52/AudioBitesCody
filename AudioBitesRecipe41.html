Lavender Lemonade

10 mins • Refreshing • 5★

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup dried lavender
  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 4 cups water

Boil water with lavender.

Strain lavender and mix with lemon juice and honey.

Chill before serving.
