Butternut Squash Soup

1 hr • Easy • 4.9★

Ingredients

  • 1 large butternut squash (peeled, seeded, cubed)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk or heavy cream (optional)
0:00 0:00

Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.

Add onion, garlic, and carrot. Cook until soft.

Add butternut squash and vegetable broth.

Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 25–30 minutes.

When the squash is tender, blend the soup until smooth (careful, it’s hot!).

Stir in nutmeg, salt, pepper, and coconut milk (if using).

Simmer another 5 minutes to warm through.

Serve hot with crusty bread or a drizzle of cream.

Perfect for chilly nights!
