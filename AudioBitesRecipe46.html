Rachael Ray's Chicken Piccata

30 minutes • Italian • 5★

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup flour, for dredging
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup capers
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper

Dredge chicken in flour.

Sear chicken in oil.

Deglaze with wine and lemon juice.

Serve with capers and garnish.
