Cake Pops

1 hr • Moderate • 4.8★

Ingredients

  • 1 baked cake (any flavor)
  • 1/2 cup frosting
  • Candy melts or chocolate for coating
  • Sprinkles or decorations
  • Lollipop sticks
0:00 0:00

Crumble the baked cake into a large bowl.

Add frosting a spoonful at a time, mixing until the texture is like dough.

Roll into balls and chill for 30 minutes.

Melt candy coating or chocolate according to package instructions.

Dip each stick into melted chocolate, then insert into a cake ball.

Dip cake balls into the melted coating and let excess drip off.

Add sprinkles before it hardens.

Let them set, serve, and enjoy!
