Ina Garten's Roast Chicken

2 hours • Classic • 5★

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4 lb)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, quartered
  • 1 onion, halved
  • Fresh rosemary and thyme
  • Salt and pepper

Season chicken with oil and herbs.

Stuff with lemon and onion.

Roast for 2 hours.

Serve with your favorite sides.
