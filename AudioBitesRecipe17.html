Chicken and Peas with Chopped Potatoes

40 min • Easy • 4.7★

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 4 medium potatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
0:00 4:00

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

Peel and chop the potatoes into bite-sized pieces.

Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.

Season chicken with paprika, thyme, salt, and pepper.

Place chicken in the pan and sear each side for 4-5 minutes, until golden.

Transfer chicken to a baking dish and bake for 15-20 minutes until cooked through.

In the same pan, add garlic and potatoes, saute for 5 minutes.

Add peas and cover with a lid. Let steam for 5 minutes.

Serve the chicken on a plate with the peas and potatoes.

Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.
