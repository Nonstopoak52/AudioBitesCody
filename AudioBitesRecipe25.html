Latkes

45 mins • Intermediate • 4.7★

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Optional toppings: sour cream, applesauce
0:00 0:00

Grate the potatoes and onion using a box grater or food processor.

Transfer to a clean towel and squeeze out as much liquid as possible.

Place the mixture in a bowl and add eggs, flour, salt, and pepper.

Mix until combined.

Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

Scoop about 2 tablespoons of mixture per latke, flatten, and fry until golden brown.

Flip and cook the other side.

Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate.

Serve warm with sour cream or applesauce.

Enjoy your crispy, savory latkes!
