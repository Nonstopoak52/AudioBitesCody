Italian Risotto

45 mins • Italian • 5★

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Sauté onion and garlic.

Add rice and wine.

Stir in stock gradually.

Finish with butter and cheese.
