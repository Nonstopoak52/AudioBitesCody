Jamie Oliver's Chicken in a Pot

1 hour 30 minutes • Comfort Food • 5★

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3.5 lb)
  • 1 onion, peeled and halved
  • 2 carrots, peeled
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 4 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1 bunch fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 lemon, halved

Place chicken in pot with vegetables.

Add garlic and thyme.

Roast for 1.5 hours.

Serve hot!
