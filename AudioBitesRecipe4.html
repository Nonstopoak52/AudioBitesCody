TacoCakes

35 min • Intermediate • 4.7★

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes (cold)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • Oil for frying
  • Toppings: sour cream, salsa, avocado
0:00 2:45

Mix cold mashed potatoes, cheese, and half the breadcrumbs in a bowl.

Brown ground beef with onions, drain fat, and add taco seasoning with 1/4 cup water. Simmer 5 minutes.

Form potato mixture into small patties. Make an indentation, add 1 tbsp taco meat, then seal with more potato.

Dip each cake in egg, then remaining breadcrumbs. Fry in oil 3-4 minutes per side until golden.

Serve hot with sour cream, salsa, and avocado slices.
