Full English Breakfast

30 min • Medium • 4.8★

Ingredients

  • 2 sausages
  • 2 slices of back bacon
  • 1 tomato, halved
  • 1 cup baked beans
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 slices of toast
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
0:00 3:00

Heat a large pan over medium heat with oil or butter.

Add sausages and cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally.

Add bacon and cook until crisp, about 4 minutes.

In a separate pan, grill tomatoes cut-side down until softened.

Add mushrooms and cook until golden.

Heat baked beans in a small pot until warm.

Fry eggs to your preference.

Toast the bread and butter if desired.

Assemble everything on a large plate and serve hot.
