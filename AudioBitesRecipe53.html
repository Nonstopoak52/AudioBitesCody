North American Lobster Roll

30 min • North American • 4.9★

Ingredients

  • 2 lobster tails, cooked and chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 4 buttered and toasted rolls
  • Salt and pepper to taste
0:00 2:00

In a bowl, combine the cooked lobster, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and parsley.

Season with salt and pepper.

Fill each buttered roll with lobster mixture and serve immediately.
