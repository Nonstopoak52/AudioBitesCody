Champagne Risotto

40 mins • Elegant • 5★

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup champagne
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cook Arborio rice in butter, add champagne and stir.

Add chicken broth, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly.

Finish with parmesan, salt, and pepper.
