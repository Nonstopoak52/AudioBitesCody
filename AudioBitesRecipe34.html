Veggie Stir-Fry with Tofu

25 mins • Easy • 5★

Ingredients

  • 1 block firm tofu (pressed and cubed)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger (grated)
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
0:00 0:00

Heat sesame oil in a pan, add tofu and cook until golden.

Remove tofu, add veggies and cook until tender.

Add ginger, garlic, hoisin sauce, and soy sauce, stir-fry together.

Serve with tofu on top and garnish with sesame seeds.
