Peppermint Bark

30 min • Super Easy • 5★

Ingredients

  • 12 oz dark chocolate (chopped or chips)
  • 12 oz white chocolate (chopped or chips)
  • 1/2 tsp peppermint extract
  • 6–8 peppermint candies or candy canes (crushed)
0:00 0:00

Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Melt the dark chocolate in a double boiler or microwave until smooth.

Stir in peppermint extract.

Pour onto the prepared sheet and spread evenly.

Refrigerate until set, about 15 minutes.

Melt the white chocolate. Let it cool slightly, then pour over the dark layer.

Sprinkle crushed peppermints on top.

Chill again until completely firm, then break into pieces.

Festive, minty, and delicious!
