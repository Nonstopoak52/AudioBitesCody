Gordon Ramsay's Beef Wellington

2 hours • Gourmet • 5★

Ingredients

  • 2 lb beef tenderloin
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 10 oz cremini mushrooms
  • 8 slices prosciutto
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten

Sear beef in olive oil until brown.

Brush with Dijon mustard.

Prepare mushrooms, wrap beef in prosciutto and pastry.

Bake until golden.
