Salted Caramel Pretzel Brownies

60 mins • Sweet • 5★

Ingredients

  • 1 box brownie mix
  • 1/2 cup pretzels (crushed)
  • 1/2 cup caramel sauce
  • 1/4 cup sea salt

Prepare brownie mix according to package instructions.

Pour into pan, top with pretzels.

Bake for 25 minutes.

Drizzle caramel sauce, sprinkle with sea salt.
