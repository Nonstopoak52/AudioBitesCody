Honey-Lavender Lemonade

30 mins • Easy • 5★

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup dried lavender flowers
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 1/2 cups freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 6 lemons)
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices and lavender sprigs for garnish
0:00 0:00

In a small saucepan, combine lavender flowers and 1/2 cup water.

Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer for 10 minutes.

Strain out lavender, then stir in honey.

In a pitcher, combine lemon juice, lavender honey mixture, and remaining water.

Add ice cubes, garnish with lemon slices and lavender sprigs.

Serve chilled and enjoy!
