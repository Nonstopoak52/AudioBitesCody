Jalapeño Wonton Poppers

30 min • Spicy • 4.6★

Ingredients

  • 12 wonton wrappers
  • 2–3 jalapeños, finely diced
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Optional: sour cream or ranch for dipping
0:00 2:30

In a bowl, mix cream cheese, cheddar, jalapeños, and garlic powder until well blended.

Spoon a small amount of filling into the center of each wonton wrapper.

Brush edges with egg wash and fold diagonally to seal.

Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.

Fry the wontons in batches until golden brown, about 2–3 minutes per side.

Drain on paper towels and serve hot with your favorite dip!
