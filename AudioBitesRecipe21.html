Fudge Pie

35 min • Easy • 4.9★

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 unbaked pie crust
0:00 0:00

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

In a bowl, combine melted butter, sugar, and eggs.

Beat well until smooth.

Add flour, cocoa, vanilla, and salt.

Mix until fully combined.

Pour into the unbaked pie crust.

Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until center is set.

Cool slightly, slice, and serve with whipped cream or ice cream.
