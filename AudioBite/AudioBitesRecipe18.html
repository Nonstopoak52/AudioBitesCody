Ribs

1 hour • Medium • 4.8★

Ingredients

  • 2 racks of pork ribs
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp ground mustard
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
0:00 2:30

Preheat your oven to 300°F.

Mix all the dry ingredients together in a bowl.

Rub the spice blend over the ribs, making sure it coats evenly.

Place the ribs on a baking sheet lined with foil.

Cover with more foil and bake for 2.5 hours.

Remove the top layer of foil and brush with BBQ sauce.

Return to the oven for another 30 minutes or until tender.

Slice and serve!
