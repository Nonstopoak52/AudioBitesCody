Egg and Hash

25 min • Easy • 4.8★

Ingredients

  • 2 medium potatoes, diced
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 eggs
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
0:00 2:15

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

Add potatoes and cook for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Add onion and bell pepper. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.

Continue cooking until vegetables are tender and browned, about 5 minutes.

Make two small wells in the hash and crack an egg into each.

Cover and cook until eggs are set to your liking, about 3-4 minutes.

Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
