Egg Chicken Salad

20 min • Easy • 4.7★

Ingredients

  • 2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 egg (soft-boiled or poached)
  • ¼ cup Caesar dressing
  • ¼ cup croutons
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
0:00 2:30

Start by soft-boiling or poaching your egg.

Set aside to cool slightly.

In a large bowl, combine the romaine lettuce, grilled chicken, and croutons.

Drizzle with Caesar dressing and toss to coat evenly.

Top with Parmesan cheese and the egg.

Season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.
