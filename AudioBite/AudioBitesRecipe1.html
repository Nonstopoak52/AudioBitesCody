Grilled Chicken Caesar Salad

25 min • Easy • 4.7★

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 romaine lettuce heart
  • ½ cup grated parmesan
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 3 tbsp Caesar dressing
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
0:00 0:39

Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.

Heat grill to medium-high and brush with olive oil. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through.

While chicken cooks, chop romaine into bite-sized pieces and place in a large bowl.

Let chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice into strips.

Add chicken, croutons, and parmesan to the lettuce. Drizzle with dressing and toss gently to combine.
