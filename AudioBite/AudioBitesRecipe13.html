Lemon Herb Chicken

40 min • Refreshing • 4.8★

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
0:00 2:45

In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.

Add chicken breasts to the mixture and marinate for at least 15 minutes.

Heat a skillet over medium heat.

Add chicken and cook for 6–7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked.

Let rest a few minutes, then slice and garnish with fresh parsley.

Serve with your favorite sides or over a bed of rice.
