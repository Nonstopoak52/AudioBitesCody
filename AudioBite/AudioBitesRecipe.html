Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

30 min • Intermediate • 4.9★

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale
  • 4 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt
0:00 0:42

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente.

Meanwhile, cut the pancetta into small cubes and fry in a pan until crispy.

In a bowl, whisk the eggs with grated cheeses and plenty of black pepper.

Drain the pasta, reserving some pasta water. Quickly mix with the pancetta, then remove from heat and add the egg mixture, stirring constantly to create a creamy sauce. Add pasta water if needed.

Serve immediately with cheese and pepper.
