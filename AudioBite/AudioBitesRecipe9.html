Protein Burrito

25 min • Medium • 4.8★

Ingredients

  • 2 large whole wheat tortillas
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup black beans (cooked)
  • ½ cup shredded chicken breast
  • ½ avocado, sliced
  • ¼ cup diced tomatoes
  • ¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hot sauce (optional)
0:00 3:00

Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.

Crack in the eggs, season with salt and pepper, and scramble until cooked through.

Warm the tortillas briefly in a dry pan or microwave.

Layer scrambled eggs, black beans, chicken, cheese, tomatoes, and avocado on each tortilla.

Top with Greek yogurt and optional hot sauce.

Fold in the sides, roll up tightly, and serve warm.
