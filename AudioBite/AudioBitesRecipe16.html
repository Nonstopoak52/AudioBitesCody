Steak and Potatoes with Fried Greens & Mushrooms

40 min • Hearty • 4.9★

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye or sirloin steaks
  • 4 medium potatoes, cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp rosemary or thyme
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed greens (kale, spinach, or chard)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce or balsamic vinegar
0:00 3:30

Boil the cubed potatoes in salted water for 10 minutes until just tender.

Drain and let them dry.

Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.

Sear the steaks 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare, or longer to your liking.

Add butter, garlic, and herbs to the pan and baste the steak for 30 seconds.

Remove steaks and let them rest.

In a separate pan, heat 1 tbsp olive oil and sauté mushrooms until golden.

Add greens and stir until wilted.

Drizzle with soy sauce or balsamic vinegar to finish.

For the potatoes, pan-fry in the same pan with butter until crispy on the edges.

Serve steak with golden potatoes and a side of greens.
